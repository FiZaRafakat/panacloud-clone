Streamlined Flows for Developers & Investors

HOW IT WORKS FOR A DEVELOPER?

1
INITIATION

Developer comes up with a startup idea and creates a DAO representing the product on Panacloud Platform.

2
SOLICITATION

Once DAO is made, the Developer will generate a request for an IDO campaign

3
APPRAISAL

Panacloud research team counsels and approves the IDO request for the next stage. Together, they decide the whitelist criteria.

4
P. COUNCIL

Whitelisted Panacoin holders receive the IDO campaign request and make the final decision.

5
LAUNCH

After the final decision is made, IDO is complete and Pool becomes ready to invest.

HOW IT WORKS FOR AN INVESTOR?

1
SELECT POOL

Investor can check all the pools ready for investment. Investor selects a pool to invest.

2
INVEST

Whitelisted Panacoin holders will invest their Panacoins and receive tokens of the DAO.

3
SUCCESS

DAO governance and revenue awarded to investors as per tokens held. If pool fails, investor can claim their panacoins back.

Multi-Token System

PANACOIN

Panacoin powers the governance protocol on Panacloud. PC holders are platform's stakeholders that can cast votes to implement business decisions

DAO TOKENS

DAO tokens represent the share of software held by the DAO. Dao's revenue is distributed through these tokens to the holders corresponding to the share they hold.
